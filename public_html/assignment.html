Getting Data into Access

This assignment has a total of 100 points.

Do not insert or modify tables, fields, or records unless instructed to do so. The grader relies on the current database structure and records for grading.

This database is a part of cookbook project. The single table is just the list of recipes. Tables holding information about ingredients and nutrition are omitted for simplicity of this practice exercise.

Working with Tables

Imagine that you want to add the ability to keep track of reviews made of the recipes. As people try the different recipes in this database, you need to provide for them a table to enter a review of how the recipe turned out. You will need to collect the date of the review, a rating (0-5 stars), and the name of the person doing the review on the new table. The tasks below will guide you through the process.
